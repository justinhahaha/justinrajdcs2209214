Lesson of the Day: Basic Grammar

Today we'll be learning some basic grammar. Here are a few rules to keep in mind:

  • Always capitalize the first letter of a sentence.
  • Use proper punctuation (commas, periods, etc.) to separate ideas and sentences.
  • Subject-verb agreement: the verb must agree with the subject in number (singular or plural).
  • Use articles (a, an, the) appropriately.
  • Avoid using run-on sentences (sentences that are too long and have multiple ideas without proper punctuation).

Practice these rules by writing a few simple sentences on your own, and make sure to review your work for any errors.
