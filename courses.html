Our Courses

Diploma in Computer Science

Duration: 2 years

Diploma in Computer Science program equips students with fundamental knowledge and skills in computer science and prepares them for careers in various technology-related fields.

Diploma in Accounting

Duration: 2 years

The Diploma in Accounting program provides students with a strong foundation in accounting principles and practices, preparing them for entry-level positions in the field or further studies in accounting.

Diploma in Business Administration

Duration: 2 years

Diploma in Business Administration provides students with fundamental knowledge and skills in areas such as marketing, finance, accounting, and management, preparing them for entry-level positions in various business industries.

Bachelor of Software Engineering

Duration: 4 years

Bachelor of Software Engineering is a four-year degree program that focuses on the principles, practices, and methodologies of software development, preparing students for careers in software engineering, software design, and related fields.

Bachelor of Laws

Duration: 4 years

Bachelor of Laws (LLB) is an undergraduate degree that prepares students for a career in law by providing a comprehensive understanding of legal principles, policies, and practices. Graduates of this program are equipped with the necessary knowledge and skills to become successful lawyers, judges, legal advisors, and policymakers.

Bachelor of Business

Duration: 4 years

Bachelor of Business is a degree program that provides students with a comprehensive understanding of various aspects of business, management, and leadership.
